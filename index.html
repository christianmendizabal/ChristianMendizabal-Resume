My name is Christian Mendizabal. I was born in San Francisco and lived in the bay area my entire life. Now that I have begun my college career, I have decided to major in Public Policy. Public Policy has interested me since highschools when I see so many traumatizing events happen with little to no change being done in government. Public Policy has shown me the different steps it takes for a policy to be implemented, the outcome of different policies, and the public's reaction to policies that we may think will help, but in the end don't.
